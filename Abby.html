Hi, my name is Abby!

I am a Catahoula Leopard Dog

What is a Catahoula Leopard Dog, you ask? Well, we are the state dog breed of Louisiana! Depending on who you ask, we are bred for:

  • Rounding up livestock in swampland
  • Hunting wild boar
  • Being adorable

Personally, I really enjoy being out in the woods and I *love* hunting squirrels and chasing livestock. Unfortunately, the goats I've met don't listen to me telling them where to go.
